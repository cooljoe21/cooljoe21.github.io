Who am I?




I am currently the president of the San Marcos High School Video Game Development Club. We work on creating video games and going through the motions that a video game company would go through, but over the course of a year. This year we are working on producing a game that will be published online for others to play for a profit. I also work on projects of my own, whether it is a Unity project or a professional website.

Current Project

I am currently the Executive Producer of Phantasmic Mind. Phantasmic Mind is a 2D RPG game where you play as Henry, a depressed man, who wanders through life looking for evil, or at least he thinks... This game is created to display the complexity that the story of a video game can have, while still being engaging and keep the video game feel.

Previous Projects

Sophmore year I was lucky enough to get the chance to lead the Video Game Club. We worked on a survival wave based FPS. The game was beyond my wildest imagination for that year, and I am proud of what we created. But, beyond the game, the experience truly opened my eyes to what being an executive producer really meant. I learned that teamwork is not something natural that comes to most people and that communication between people, or groups of people is key.
